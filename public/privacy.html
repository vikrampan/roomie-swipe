Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

At RoomieSwipe ("we," "our," or "us"), we operate on a principle of "Data Minimalism." We only collect what is strictly necessary to help you find a roommate or a room.

Community Initiative
Note on Entity Status: RoomieSwipe is currently an independent, open-source inspired community project designed to help students and professionals find housing. We are not yet a registered corporation or LLC. This service is provided "as-is" for the benefit of the community.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following categories of data:

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Data: When you sign up via Google or Phone, we collect your name, email address, profile photo, and phone number (if verified).
  • Profile Information: Age, gender, occupation, budget, lifestyle habits (e.g., smoking, drinking, sleep schedule), and bio.
  • User Content: Photos of yourself or your room/apartment that you upload to our secure storage.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Geolocation Data: We collect your precise latitude and longitude to calculate the distance between you and potential matches. This is stored as a "Geohash" for efficient querying.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including swipes ("Likes" or "Passes"), matches, and time spent on the platform.
  • Device Information: IP address, device model, and operating system version for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for specific business purposes:

  • Matching Algorithm: To calculate "Vibe Scoresâ„¢" and display profiles within your specified radius and budget.
  • Communication: To facilitate secure, in-app messaging between mutual matches without revealing personal contact details.
  • Security: To verify accounts (via OTP) and prevent fraudulent behavior or harassment.
  • Service Improvements: To analyze aggregate trends (e.g., "Most popular locations") to improve our app infrastructure.

3. Affiliate & Monetization Disclosure

RoomieSwipe is a free-to-use community tool. To cover our server costs (Firebase, Maps API), we participate in affiliate marketing programs.

We may display "Smart Cards" or advertisements within your feed for relevant services (e.g., furniture rental, packers & movers). These links contain tracking cookies. If you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission to support the project at no cost to you.

Note: We do not share your personal identity (name/email) with these advertisers. They only receive an anonymous signal that a click originated from our app.

4. Data Retention & The "Right to be Forgotten"

We respect your right to control your digital footprint. Unlike other platforms that "soft delete" data, we enforce a strict "Clean Sweep" Deletion Policy.

If you choose to delete your account via the Settings menu:

  • Profile Removal: Your user document is immediately and permanently deleted from our Cloud Firestore database.
  • Media Wipe: Your photos are physically deleted from our Firebase Storage buckets.
  • Interaction Purge: All "Likes" you sent and all "Matches" you created are deleted. You will disappear from other users' chat lists and "Who Liked Me" views.

Once this process is triggered, it cannot be undone. We do not retain backups of deleted profiles.

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted via HTTPS/SSL and encrypted at rest within Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Canvas Image Protection: We utilize proprietary canvas-rendering technology to display user images. This prevents standard "right-click to save" actions and automated scraping, ensuring your photos remain within the app environment.
  • Access Control: Strict database security rules ensure that no user can read or write data belonging to another user, except for mutually matched public profile information.

6. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party processors:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, hosting, database, and storage services.
  • Google Maps Platform / Geofire: For location processing and geocoding.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is strictly restricted to users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect PII from minors. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account and associated data.

8. Liability & Community Nature

As an independent project, we strive for uptime and security but provide the service on an "AS IS" basis. We are not a regulated real estate provider. Please exercise caution and common sense when meeting strangers.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or via email.
